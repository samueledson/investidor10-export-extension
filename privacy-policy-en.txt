Privacy policy — Investidor10 Export (Chrome extension)

Last updated: 2026-07-08

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to the developer or third parties.

What the extension does
- When you use the popup, the extension reads the investment positions already displayed on the Investidor10 wallet positions page.
- It can expand the visible asset type tables and export the rendered position data as CSV or PDF files.
- CSV and PDF generation happens locally in your browser.

Data and permissions
- The extension may read the current tab's URL only to confirm that you are on a supported Investidor10 wallet positions page.
- The extension reads table text rendered on the page, such as asset names, quantities, prices, balances, percentages, and other visible columns, only to generate the export files requested by you.
- The extension does not log, upload, sell, or share your wallet data.
- The extension does not use external servers, analytics SDKs, or remote processing.

Third parties
- The extension runs locally in Chrome and does not send data to the developer or third parties.
- The Investidor10 website itself is operated by Investidor10 and is subject to its own privacy policy.

Contact
- Project repository: https://github.com/samueledson/investidor10-export-extension

Host this document at a public HTTPS URL and enter that URL in the Chrome Web Store developer dashboard under Privacy practices.
